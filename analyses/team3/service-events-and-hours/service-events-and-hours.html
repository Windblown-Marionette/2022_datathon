morning_hours = [str(hour) + 'am' for hour in range (1,12)]
evening_hours = [str(hour) + 'pm' for hour in range (1,12)]
day_hours = morning_hours
day_hours = ['12am'] + day_hours
day_hours = day_hours + ['12pm']
day_hours.extend(evening_hours)
print(day_hours)

A notable handful of events took place outside of these busy hours. Using the food pantry web page, we can find the following service hours.

ElderNet’s Ada Mutch Food Pantry Hours of Operation: Tuesdays and Thursdays 10 a.m. to 11:45 a.m. or 2:00 p.m. to 3:45 p.m.

ElderNet Hours of Operation: Monday through Friday 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

This suggests that either data has been incorrectly entered, or some employees are going above and beyond to help provide access to food off-hours.
